About Us

About Q.C.I

Leading Quality Analysis & Inspection Company

Quality & Container Inspection LTD (Q.C.I) is a premier company offering comprehensive third-party services in quality analysis and inspection across multiple industries.

We specialize in quality analysis for agricultural produces including coffee, cocoa, maize, rice, sorghum, and beans. Our expertise extends to sourcing both green specialty and commercial coffee in Uganda, providing thorough container inspection services, conducting stock counts/audits and verifications, and offering professional fumigation services.

Q.C.I is dedicated to timely delivery and maintains the highest standards of professionalism in all our services, ensuring our clients receive accurate, reliable, and comprehensive inspection and analysis solutions.
